# Explanation:
Prompting is the method of giving instructions to an AI model to generate responses. The clarity and structure of prompts can significantly affect the quality of the output.

In this experiment, two types of prompts are analyzed:

## 1. Naïve Prompt

A naïve prompt is broad, simple, and unstructured. It gives minimal instructions to the AI model.

Example:
Write a story about a beach.

Such prompts often produce general responses with limited detail.

## 2. Basic Prompt

A basic prompt is clear, structured, and detailed, providing specific instructions or context to guide the AI model.

Example:
Write a short creative story about a small girl flying a kite on a beach with ocean waves during sunset.

These prompts generally produce better organized and more informative responses.

## Write a Basic Prompt:
A basic prompt includes clear instructions, context, and expected output format.

Example Basic Prompt:

Explain Artificial Intelligence in detail for a second-year IT student. Include definition, types, applications, advantages, and limitations in about 150–200 words.

This prompt guides the AI to generate structured and detailed information.
## Scenario 1: Creative Story Generation:
Naïve Prompt

Write a story about a beach.

Observed Response

The AI generated a simple story about a beach and people enjoying the scenery. The story was short and less descriptive.

Basic Prompt

Write a short creative story about a small girl flying a kite on a beach with ocean waves during sunset.

Observed Response

The AI generated a more detailed story describing the ocean waves, the wind, the girl’s excitement, and the sunset scenery.
## Scenario 2: Answering a Factual Question
Naïve Prompt

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Observed Response

A short definition explaining that Artificial Intelligence enables machines to perform tasks requiring human intelligence.

Basic Prompt

Explain Artificial Intelligence including definition, types, applications, advantages, and limitations.

Observed Response

The AI produced a structured answer with clear sections and detailed explanation.
## Scenario 3: Summarizing a Concept
Naïve Prompt

Summarize Machine Learning.

Observed Response

A short summary describing Machine Learning in a few sentences.

Basic Prompt

Summarize the concept of Machine Learning in 5–6 lines including its purpose and real-world applications.

Observed Response

A clearer summary including the purpose, learning process, and practical applications.
## Scenario 4: Providing Advice
Naïve Prompt

Give tips for studying.

Observed Response

General tips such as concentrating, revising regularly, and avoiding distractions.

Basic Prompt

Provide five effective study tips for college students preparing for semester exams.

Observed Response

More structured advice including time management, note-taking, practice tests, revision planning, and regular breaks.

## Analysis:
The experiment shows that prompt clarity strongly influences the quality of AI-generated responses.

Naïve prompts generate short and general responses.

Basic prompts produce more structured, detailed, and informative outputs.

For simple questions, naïve prompts may still provide acceptable answers.

For complex tasks like storytelling, summarization, and recommendations, basic prompts perform significantly better.
